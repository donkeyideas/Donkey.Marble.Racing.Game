1. Pebble Hill
Beginner • 45s • Green Meadow
Classic zigzag. 5 platforms, gentle slopes. One obstacle per platform. The training course.
2. Lava Drop
Intermediate • 55s • Volcano
Plinko-style vertical drop. Peg rows scatter marbles, spinning hammer creates chaos. Funnel finish forces single-file.
3. Pinball Alley
Intermediate • 50s • Arcade
Bumper-heavy course. Marbles bounce between bumper clusters on each platform. Total chaos — luck matters most.
4. The Fork
Intermediate • 50s • Canyon
Splitter at mid-course forces marbles left or right. Two different paths rejoin at the bottom. Which path is faster?
5. Tornado Alley
Hard • 55s • Storm
3 spinners on consecutive platforms. Cascading knockbacks create massive position swings. Bouncy marbles thrive.
6. The Gauntlet
Hard • 60s • Dungeon
Narrow gates + moving blocks on every platform. Forces single-file passing. Power stat dominates.
7. Trampoline Park
Intermediate • 50s • Playground
Jump ramps launch marbles between platforms. Marbles fly through the air, skip platforms. Bounce stat is king.
8. Ice Slide
Hard • 40s • Frozen
Ultra-low friction platforms. Marbles go FAST. Speed pads on every ramp. Short but intense — fastest race in the game.
9. Plinko Madness
Hard • 55s • Casino
Pure vertical Plinko. No platforms — just rows and rows of pegs. Marbles bounce their way down. Maximum randomness.
10. Grand Prix
Expert • 60s • Championship
Everything combined. Zigzag platforms, splitter, spinners, bumper field, speed pads, funnel finish. The ultimate test.

Each course fits in 400x1200 engine units (scrolls on phone).
