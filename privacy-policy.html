Privacy Policy

Donkey Marble Racing — Last updated: May 15, 2026

Donkey Ideas LLC ("we", "our", "us") operates the Donkey Marble Racing mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Donkey Marble Racing is designed to be privacy-friendly. We collect minimal data:

  • Game Data: Your game progress, coin balance, race history, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data to our servers.
  • Purchase Data: If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as app launches, crash reports, and feature usage) through platform-provided analytics services (Apple App Analytics, Google Firebase Analytics) to improve the app experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (device model, OS version, screen size) for compatibility and performance optimization purposes.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the App
  • To process in-app purchases through Apple/Google
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To understand general usage patterns (anonymized data only)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with:

  • Apple/Google: Platform analytics and purchase processing
  • Service Providers: Crash reporting and analytics tools that help us improve the App

4. Data Storage and Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on external servers. If you uninstall the App, your local game data will be deleted.

5. Children's Privacy

Donkey Marble Racing is not directed at children under 13. The App uses virtual currency for simulated betting within the game. No real money is wagered, won, or lost through gameplay. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases for virtual coin packs and season passes. All purchases use virtual currency only and do not constitute real-money gambling. Purchases are processed through the respective app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store).

7. Your Rights

Since we store data locally on your device, you have full control over your data:

  • You can delete all game data by uninstalling the App
  • You can contact us to request information about any data we may have

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
